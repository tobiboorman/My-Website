During my undergraduate degree, myself and a couple of fellow STEM students, ran a radio show called The Atomic Fridge (A small jest over a particular scene in Indiana Jones). The idea of the show was to bring on staff and PhD students, and allow them to discuss their work in a manner suitable for a public audience. To our delight, we found that staff and PhD students were eager to contribute, at one point we were even able to get the head of Physics and the head of Mathematics on for a “Maths vs Physics” special (see below).
